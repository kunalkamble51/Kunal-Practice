Self KYC Onboarding SIM
Traditionally, obtaining a SIM card required customers to visit a nearby Jio center, where they would need to complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process in person. However, with the introduction of the Self KYC onboarding system, customers can now acquire a physical SIM or an eSIM directly from the comfort of their homes.

Key Features

  1. Convenient Onboarding: Customers can initiate the onboarding process from their mobile devices or computers, eliminating the need for physical store visits.
  2. Aadhaar-Based Verification: The onboarding process leverages the Aadhaar card as a secure form of identity verification. Customers simply need to enter their Aadhaar details to authenticate their identity.
  3. Instant Activation Once the KYC verification is completed, the new Jio connection—either a physical SIM card or an eSIM—can be activated almost instantly. Customers receive confirmation and instructions via SMS or email.
  4. User-Friendly Interface: The onboarding platform is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate, ensuring that customers can complete the process with minimal hassle.
  5. Option for Physical SIM or eSIM: Customers can choose between receiving a physical SIM card, which will be delivered to their home, or opting for an eSIM, which can be activated immediately on compatible devices.
